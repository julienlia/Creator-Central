What Is Multimedia?

Multimedia isn't something specific, it is actually a range of different ways of expressing ideas through the use of computer programs and the internet. Anything that you create digitally, whether it be a photo you take, a website you create, or even a simple status update you upload onto a social media platform, can be associated with the term 'Multimedia'.
